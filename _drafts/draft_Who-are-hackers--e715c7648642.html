Who are hackers?

An argument for and against money in student Hackathons

What are the priorities you consider when determining whether to go to a hackathon or not?

A lot of people seem to make that decision based on a similar set of factors. I won’t claim to to know all of them, but from what I’ve seen, these are the things that seem to make the biggest differences.

Corporate Sponsors

Number Of Attendees

Prizes

Job opportunities

Free stuff

Learn and Build cool things


If you can see where I’m going with this, it’s that there’s a divide between the motives of hackers. 

There are enough different hackathons now that you can decide “who” you want to be, in a way .


* we’ll table the issue of student’s choosing between hackathons based on spacial locality, because it’s just beside the point I’m making.
